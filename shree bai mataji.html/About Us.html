About SBM WOOd WORKS

Established in 1989 Shri Mathurbhai Pankhania in Goa, Shree Bai Mataji Wood Works earned a milestone in providing the masses with rich experience in the areas of interior and furnishing. With more than 36 years of providing services, we are the most trusted brand recommended by contractors across Goa. Apart from furnishing solutions, we deliver imported Timber and panel products to various Industrial sectors. Having the latest machinery, financial resources, Experienced workers, and Skilled craftsmen in a cocoon, we have a history of completing our projects on time. Our customer base shows the strength we hold in meeting the needs and requirements efficiently. We aim to provide solutions to all your requirements while keeping up with the changing times. We have also extended our ties with professionals in allied fields like CNC Work, Interior, Modular Fabrication,Wood Polishing etc. to provide value-added services for our customers under one roof.
